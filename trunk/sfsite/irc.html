Bochs Chat on Tuesday June 19, 2001

Please join the Bochs community for an open discussion this coming Tuesday (6/19/2001). The discussion will take place between 6:00PM and 8:00PM, US Pacific time (GMT-700). Anyone who is interested may join the #Bochs channel on Undernet during this period. Topics will include
  • How is the latest release working on different platforms?
  • Who uses Bochs anyway?
  • Vote on what are the most important bugs and features
  • Plans for a new Bochs GUI. Who will work on the GUI, what libraries will be used, how will it communicate with the simulator?
  • Implementing Networking in Bochs
  • Suggestions for the SourceForge website, documentation
  • Whatever else comes up
