What is IRC and how do I get it?

There are many programs that allow you to access IRC. Many windows users use a client program called MIRC. Just do a search for "irc" on www.yahoo.com or your favorite search engine, and you will find many references to it. One site devoted to learning how to use IRC is called www.newircusers.com.
